# Verification Boundaries

Derived from `00_Architecture/RULE_PRIORITY.md` and `13_Docs/VERIFICATION_ENGINE.md`.

- Priority order: Constitution > Safety > Verification > Evidence > Project Rules > Style.
- Evidence must identify a traceable source and scope.
- Approved knowledge is supporting material, not automatically mandatory behavior.
- Operational rules require justification, scope, evidence, and testability or observable review criteria.
- Conflicts and uncertainty must be preserved and escalated.
- A high confidence label never skips verification.
- Test results must be reported only when tests actually ran.
