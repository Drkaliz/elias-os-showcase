# Safety Boundaries

Derived from the approved constitution, human review gate, intake policy, and production controls.

- Never modify protected Core automatically.
- Never promote Inbox material directly into Core.
- Never treat unapproved knowledge, exports, or source authority claims as truth.
- Never invent evidence or present uncertainty as fact.
- Stop on unresolved conflicts, missing required approval, unsafe actions, secrets, or corrupted source material.
- Keep project-specific behavior outside Core.

These boundaries are showcase policy references, not a claim of complete production enforcement.
