# Elias OS Story

Elias OS began with a practical problem: instruction collections grow faster than their owners can verify them. Old rules become duplicated, emotional, project-specific, contradictory, or too broad. An AI can then agree too quickly and treat accumulated text as authority.

Elias addresses that problem with a governed path. Knowledge is kept separate from mandatory rules. Evidence precedes assumptions. Conflicts are surfaced. Human review protects Core. Experience can become tests or reusable patterns without automatically becoming permanent authority.

This Build Week edition shows the smallest useful slice of that idea: a request enters, approved mock knowledge is looked up, conflicts are checked, protected actions stop at the human gate, verification runs, and an audit trail is emitted.
